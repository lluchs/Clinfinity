Drone

Flying robot for drilling solid material.

A drone needs a “home quarry” where it asks for coordinates of material veins and where it delivers the drilled products.  After receiving a set of coordinates, the drone will move there and search a place to drill as nearby as possible.  It will drill the material and collect the resulting objects.  When a configurable number of these objects has been collected, the drone will fly back to its quarry and deliver the objects there.
