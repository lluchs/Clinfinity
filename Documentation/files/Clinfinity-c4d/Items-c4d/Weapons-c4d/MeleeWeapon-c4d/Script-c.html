Knock back

”Knock back” pushes targets away from a weapon, or the user of the weapon, etc.  It is ensured that they do not get stuck by being moved.  If targets are knocked back on the ground, their kneeling up animation is set to disable them for a very short period of time.

Throw back

”Throw back” is worse than a simple knockback: Instead of just pushing targets away from the user of the weapon, this sends them flying away.  Usually, targets get thrown further away than knocked back, which poses an additional threat, e.g. to fall off a cliff.  Throw back can either make the targets jump, so for example Clonks can still use weapons or their wing suit, or even make them tumble.
