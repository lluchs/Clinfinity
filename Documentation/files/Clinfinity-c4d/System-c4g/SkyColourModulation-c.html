SkyColourModulation.c

Provides individual colour modulation layers for the sky background.  Layers can be drawn additively or subtractively.

With this mechanism, you can apply several different modulations without worrying about their combination yourself.  For example, a magic spell can simply set the colours of the appropriate layer, without having to consider other effects on the sky, such as night and day, lightning, etc.

For this, ten layers are available.  Generally, lower layer indices mean longer colour changess, while higher indices mean shorter effects.  Their exact purpose is summarised by the following table.  You should generally abide by these conventions, it is not mandatory, though.

Layer   | Purpose
--------+----------------------
0       | Scenario global value
1       | Climate
2       | Season
3       | Free
4       | Day/night cycle
5       | Twilight effects
6       | Free
7       | Lightning
8       | Magic effects
9       | Free
