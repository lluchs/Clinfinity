DrawResource.c

Draws a resource vein.  The algorithm draws a vein in consecutive triangles, clockwise, around a central point.

This algorithm was originally implemented by Zapper.

Draws a resource vein around a central point.  The size parameters determine the side lengths of the drawn triangles.

Parameters

materialIndexMaterial the vein will be made of.
materialTextureThe material’s texture.
tunnelBackgroundIf true, the vein will be drawn as ‘underground’.
xHorizontal coordinate of the centre.
yVertical coordinate of the centre.
minSize[optional] Minimal side length.
maxSize[optional] Maximal side length.
drawingCycles[optional] Number of full cycles the vein is drawn.
